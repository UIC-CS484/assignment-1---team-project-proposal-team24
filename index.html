Rain Weather
I will use the One Call API and the API from https://openweathermap.org/current

I plan to get data about the amount of rain that has occured or will occur in the day.
This can help farmers determine how much water they have to give to their crops as well as give information to everyday users

I will collect the latitude and longitude from the authenticated users to determine where to get the weather from.

USER -> username, password, latitude, longitude
The latitude and longitude will be the values they entered from last time.
Everyday people and farmers who need to know how much it will rain so they know how much to water their crops will use this site.
